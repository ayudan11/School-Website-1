Our Vision

Our Vision

We at DAV Public School, Sector 14, Gurugram, strive to foster in our children • Academic excellence and holistic development • Intercultural awareness and international tolerance • Acceptance and appreciation of global diversity • A sensitivity towards environmental conservation • Awareness about issues of global concern • An inclination to work collaboratively towards resolving such issues Towards this end, we endeavour to: • Present an environment that is conducive to students’ all round development • Continually improve the educational services provided by us • Brainstorm feasible methods for sustainable development • Provide relevant education and experiential learning in a changing world scenario • Highlight the international dimension within the existing curriculum • Organize curricular and co-curricular activities effectively to expand horizons

Our Mission

Mission

The DAV Mission of education is in tandem with the National Policy of Education which lays great emphasis on developing a national system of education, with education for all, keeping in mind the elimination of disparities in the education system and provision of more facilities through qualitative intervention, empowerment of women, access to education to disadvantaged sections of the society, educationally backward minorities and the disabled. It also calls for greater rigour and discipline in academic pursuits, autonomy and accountability, experimentation and innovation and nurturing excellence and modernization of processes at different levels of education. In order to accomplish the mission, the objectives laid down are as under: To provide a holistic education by homogenizing the western knowledge while remaining anchored to the Indian cultural moorings; • To act as a catalyst of change by spreading education, and by dismantling the cobwebs of ignorance and illiteracy; • To develop individuals who are morally upright, intellectually well-informed, socially concerned, emotionally balanced, physically well-developed and culturally accomplished; • To stimulate a scientific temper by crusading against superstitions and out-dated customs like child marriage, caste system, female foeticide, dowry, gender bias, regionalism etc; • To sensitize individuals towards social welfare and • To nurture creative and resourceful minds who think big, think fast and think ahead, who care for the nation and the weaker sections of society, and are imbued with humanistic passions and values.

About The Principal

About The Principal

The Principal of D.A.V. Public School, Sector-14, Gurugram Mrs. Aparna Erry has to her credit an immense reservoir of administrative experience of more than 20 years. An alumna of Lady Shriram College and postgraduate in Economics from the prestigious Delhi School of Economics, she joined DAV Public School, Sector-14, Gurugram as PGT (Economics) in 1999, and gained recognition quite early in her career for displaying exceptional administrative and managerial skills. She has enormous experience in the field of senior school education and has attended numerous workshops and conferences on modern trends in education in India and abroad. She got the opportunity to visit Russia on an educational tour under the Cultural Exchange Programme that enriched her with wide educational experience. She was also deeply involved in the initial implementation of the CCE system and offered her valuable insights in various schools as a Resource Person. Being a Subject expert in Economics, she has been a vital contributor to CBSE for imperative curriculum inputs. She attended a Programme on Strategic Leadership for school in a changing environment at IIM, Ahmedabad in October 2016, apart from attending numerous conclaves by ICTRC and FICCI. She has been certified “Digital Leader in Education” by International Center for Leadership in Education and EdTechReview.
